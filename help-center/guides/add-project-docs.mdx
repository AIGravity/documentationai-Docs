## Create a Document

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a project">
    Go to your Projects and select the project where you want to add documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Document'">
    Find the button at the top of your docs list and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write content">
    Enter a title and start writing with our editor. Add sections as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Hit "Save" to publish the document. It’s now visible to your team!
  </Step>
</Steps>
