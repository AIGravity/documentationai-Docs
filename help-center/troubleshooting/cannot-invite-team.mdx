---
title: Cannot Invite Team Members
description: Fix invite failures in Gravity.
---

## Issue

- Unable to send team invitations
- Error: "Invite failed" or no email received

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check email address">
    Make sure the address is correct and not already a member.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resend invite">
    On the Members page, click the resend icon next to the pending invite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check spam folder">
    Recipients should check their spam or junk folder for the invitation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
