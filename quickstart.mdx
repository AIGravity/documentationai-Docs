## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account creation
- Optional: Google account for SSO login
</Callout>

Gravity requires no downloads. Access everything through your browser at `https://app.gravity.com`.

## Sign Up and Log In

Begin by creating your account. Gravity supports multiple authentication methods for flexibility.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email Signup" icon="mail">
    Visit `https://app.gravity.com/signup` and follow these steps:
    
    1. Enter your email and create a password.
    2. Verify your email via the confirmation link.
    3. Log in with your credentials.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Google SSO" icon="apple">
    Click "Continue with Google" on the login page.
    
    Gravity securely handles OAuth flow without storing your Google password.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
Save your login details securely. Use a password manager for strong passwords.
</Callout>

## Create Your Workspace

Workspaces organize your project documentation. Set up your first one in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Workspaces" icon="home">
    After login, click the **Workspaces** icon in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create New Workspace" icon="plus">
    Select **New Workspace** and enter a name like "My Project Docs".
    
    Choose a description: "Documentation for my latest project".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize Settings" icon="settings">
    Set visibility to **Private** or **Public**. Add team members if needed.
    
    Click **Create** to finish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Document

Documents are MDX-powered pages. Start with a quick example.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Document" icon="file-plus">
    In your workspace, click **New Document**.
    
    Name it "Introduction".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit Content" icon="edit-3">
    Use the editor to add frontmatter and content. Here's a starter template:
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX">
    ````mdx
    ---
    title: Introduction
    description: Welcome to my project
    ---
    
    ## Overview
    
    This is your first document.
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Preview and Publish" icon="eye">
    Toggle preview mode. Click **Publish** to make it live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
